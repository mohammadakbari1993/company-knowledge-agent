# Evaluation Cases

This section defines test cases used to validate the correctness and quality of the knowledge bot responses.

The goal is to ensure that the system retrieves accurate, relevant, and concise answers from the documentation.

---

## Test Case 1 — Leave Policy

**Question:**
How many annual leave days do employees receive?

**Expected Answer:**
20 days of paid annual leave per year.

**Source:**
leave-policy.md

---

## Test Case 2 — Leave Carry Over

**Question:**

**Expected Answer:**
Yes. Up to 5 unused leave days can be carried over into the next year.

leave-policy.md
